## â“ FAQ

### Q: Where is my draft saved?
**A**: In your browser's local storage. It's private to your computer and browser.

### Q: Can I access my draft on another computer?
**A**: No, drafts are browser-specific. But you can export as JSON and import on another computer.

### Q: What happens if I clear my browser data?
**A**: Drafts will be deleted. Save as PDF or export JSON for backup.

### Q: Can multiple investigators share a draft?
**A**: No, each browser has its own draft. Use Print/PDF to share completed forms.

### Q: Do signatures work on mobile?
**A**: Yes! Draw with your finger on the touchscreen.

### Q: Can I edit after printing to PDF?
**A**: The PDF is final. To make changes, load the draft, edit, and print again.

### Q: How many drafts can I save?
**A**: One draft per browser. It's always the most recent version.

### Q: Is the draft secure?
**A**: It's stored locally on your computer only. Not sent to any server until you print/save.
